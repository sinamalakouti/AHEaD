Culture in Action: Evaluating Text-to-Image Models through Social Activities

Sina Malakouti, Boqing Gong, Adriana Kovashka
University of Pittsburgh and Boston University
ICLR 2026
Code

Abstract

Cultural nuances are best captured through social interactions, yet current text-to-image (T2I) benchmarks focus largely on object-centric artifacts. We introduce CULTIVate and AHEaD to evaluate cultural faithfulness in T2I models across alignment, hallucination, exaggeration, and diversity.
